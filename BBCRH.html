Welcome to Bikini Beach Cat Rescue

This is the homepage, feel free to look around by clicking the "Browse" tab!


Organization: Bikini Beach Cat Rescue is a tax exempt non-profit 501(c) 3 organization, incorporated in August, 2011. All contributions are tax deductible to you.

Mission: to address the problem of the free-roaming, homeless cats and kittens by offering low-cost spay/neuter clinics, and by utilizing TNR (Trap/Neuter/Return) for community (feral) cats, which reduces their numbers through natural attrition.

Why: since all shelters are full, there is a need to reduce the intake of all cats and kittens, companion and community (feral) cats.

How: by offering affordable spay/neuter clinics to the community. Please help by supporting our mission with a contribution to continue these affordable spay/neuter clinics. We cannot do this alone.e.
